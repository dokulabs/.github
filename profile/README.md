# Doku: Open Source LLM Observability

## What is Doku?

Welcome to Doku, an open-source observability tool designed to collect and understand the usage of Large Language Models (LLMs). Our tooling is crafted for enthusiasts and developers seeking to monitor and understand their LLM interactions more deeply.

## Key Features

- **Dokumetry SDKs**: Lightweight and easy-to-integrate SDKs aimed at simplifying the collection of LLM usage data.
- **Low Latency**: The `dokumetry` SDKs and Doku Platform are built with latency at mind and can process your LLM call usage data in small time
- **Visualization**: Doku's platform translates raw data into comprehensible visual dashboards tailored for LLMs.
and understanding of your LLM usage.
- **Data Exportability**: Flexibility to export data to your preferred observability tools, like Grafana Cloud, while still reaping the tailored benefits of Doku's specialized LLM observability features.
